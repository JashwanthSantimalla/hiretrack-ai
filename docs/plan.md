# HireTrack AI

## Project Vision

HireTrack AI is an AI-powered Applicant Tracking System (ATS) that helps recruiters identify the best candidates by matching resumes against a job description.

Instead of manually reviewing hundreds of resumes, recruiters simply paste a job description, upload candidate resumes, and let AI analyze, compare, and rank applicants based on how well they match the role.

Our goal is to reduce recruitment time while improving hiring quality.

---

## Problem Statement

Recruiters often receive hundreds of resumes for a single job opening.

Reviewing every resume manually is time-consuming, repetitive, and often leads to missed opportunities.

Current Applicant Tracking Systems mainly store resumes but do not intelligently compare candidates against job requirements.

HireTrack AI solves this problem by automatically analyzing resumes, extracting skills and experience, comparing candidates with the job description, and providing intelligent hiring recommendations.

---

## Target Users

- Small & Medium Businesses (SMBs)
- Startups
- HR Teams
- Recruiters
- Hiring Managers

---

## Version 1 Goal

Build an AI-powered recruitment platform that enables recruiters to:

- Create a job opening
- Paste a job description
- Upload one or more resumes
- Automatically analyze resumes
- Match candidates against the job description
- View AI Match Scores
- Identify missing skills
- Receive hiring recommendations
- Shortlist the best candidates quickly
